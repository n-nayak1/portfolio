About Me

I am a designer with an electromechanical toolkit, focused on tackling everyday problems in an end-to-end process I refer to as “design-gineering.”

My study of mechanical and electrical engineering, coupled with my industry experience in R&D, Engineering, and Manufacturing, has helped me develop a thorough understanding of what it takes for a product to go from an idea to a mass-produced commodity.

What is
design-gineering?

A design-gineer understands end-to-end product development, from ideation through design / prototyping to a final product that can be mass-produced.
